Jefferson Davis

The first, and only, President of the Confederate States. Davis, representing the South, was found of slavery. However, Davis had poor leadership skills, which ultimately led to the fall of the Confederate States.

Plantation Owners

Plantation owners made a huge profit while using slaves. The slaves provided free work, which led to more of a profit. The plantation owners were in favor of slavery because of all the profit that existed while using slaves. Just like slave traders, plantation owners even fought for their slaves, so they could keep making money.

Slave Traders

Just like plantation owners, slave traders made a huge profit on buying and selling slaves. These people were in favor of slavery because of all the profit that existed within slaves. Slave traders even fought for their slaves, so they could keep making money.

Slave Owners

Slave owners would typically treat their slaves terribly. As shown in this picture, the slaves would get whipped, or even beaten. Some slaves would even get killed while enduring their punishment.
