Abolitionism

Abolitionism is the belief that slavery should be abolished. In the early nineteenth century, increasing numbers of people in the northern United States believed that the nation's slaves should be freed; this would occur without compensation to slave owners.

Abolitionists

First off, an abolitionist is someone who favors the abolition of a practice or institution; this especially being slavery.
